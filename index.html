How will 2020 Independence Day be?

This year Independence Day celebrations will be markedly different due to the novel coronavirus outbreak.
The Independence Day function at the Red Fort is going to be carried out
with extra precautions pertaining to health as compared to the ones held in the earlier years.
But the presence of school children will most likely be absent from the function this year, reports the Indian Express.
The decision has not been conveyed officially as yet.
